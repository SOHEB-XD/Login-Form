    About Me.

    Hey! I'm Soheb Khan — a full-time web developer and part-time chai enthusiast. I build websites that actually work (unlike my sleep schedule) and make pixels behave with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

    I spend most of my time fighting bugs, Googling error messages like a pro, and pretending I know

    what I’m doing — and surprisingly, it works! From sleek designs to smooth interactions, I’m obsessed

    with making your screen look sexy.

    Outside the screen? You’ll find me planning my next project, eating snacks I didn’t need, or explaining to my mom that “yes, this is a real job.”

    Let’s code, laugh, and build something amazing — preferably before my coffee wears off!
